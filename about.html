More About Me As a Developer

I am a front end/web developer from Bosnia and Herzegovina. I focus mostly on web stack (HTML5, CSS3, JS). I am a great fan of React library as a choice, although I like Angular as well.

Although I am passionately devoted to front-end development, I have great interest in backend technologies like NodeJS, Express, any databases.

Short Bio

I was bron in a small municipality Vogošća, near Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. I finished four years of elementary school there, and the other four in Višegrad. I finished high school in Višegrad as well.

I graduated from the University of East Sarajevo, School of Philosophy in 2008 with a Bachelors Degree in English Language and Literature.

After a few small part-time summer jobs. I landed the job of a translator at a local TV station where I still work.
